Fabiana: A Family Journey Chasing Their Olympic Dream ✨

In 2022, Fabiana, her husband, and their two sons took a bold and exciting step: they moved from Brazil to California. The reason? To support their sons, who are professional skateboarders competing in championships around the globe.

Before this big move, Fabiana and her husband were successful entrepreneurs and owned a skate shop in Brazil. However, when their sons dream of becoming Olympic skateboarders started to take shape, they decided it was time for a major life change.

“It was a big decision, but we've never regretted it,” Fabiana shares. For her and her husband, the greatest joy is watching their sons do what they love and shine in competitions.

This story isn't just about sacrifice; it's about making intentional choices in the name of love and purpose. Fabiana and her family show that with unity, dreams can become reality — and in the end, every effort is worth it.
